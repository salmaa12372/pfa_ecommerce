Our Heritage

From Carthage
to Bena

A recipe passed through the souks of Tunis, revived with love and intention.

Bena revives the lost art of Tunisian bread-making. Using organic barley, sesame from Sfax, and unfiltered Jebel honey, each bite is slow-baked in traditional stone ovens. No additives, no shortcuts — just the honest taste of North Africa.

Every batch is handcrafted by local bakers in Sidi Bou Saïd, ensuring a perfect crunch and a golden hue that mirrors the Tunisian sun over the Gulf of Tunis.
